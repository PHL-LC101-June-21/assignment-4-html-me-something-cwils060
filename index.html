I was born and raised in Richmond, VA. Richmond is a city full of southern charm and character.

Fun Facts About Richmond:

  • Richmond is one of two places in North America with triple crossing railroad tracks.
  • Its a great place for whitewater rafting - its the only city in the US with class IV rapids
  • You'll find that a neighborhood called Jackson Ward used to be known as the “Harlem of the South” - a thriving community for black culture and businesses

Places in Richmond I liked to vist:

  1. Virginia Museum of Fine Arts
  2. Carytown - shopping district full of unique and eclectic boutique shops

What did I do while I was in Richmond?

For 4 years from 2015 - 2019, I worked at a financial technology company that focused on loans and credit cards. Their goal was to help people build their credit who may not have had access to traditional forms of credit or no credit at all.

Moving in 2019

Where am I now?

In November 2019 I moved to Philly. I currently live in Germantown and absolutely love its rich history, unique architecture, and vibrant culture.

What do I do now?
